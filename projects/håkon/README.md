# Reading Log

A simple app for tracking the books in your life — the ones you've finished, the one you're in the middle of, and the pile you want to get to. Built for any reader who wants ratings and notes in one place instead of scattered across apps.

## What it should do

- Add a book with title, author, and a status: *want to read*, *reading*, or *read*
- Move a book between the three statuses as you progress through it
- Give finished books a rating (e.g. 1–5 stars)
- Jot down a short note or review for each book
- See your books grouped by status at a glance
