# Reading Log

A personal app for tracking your reading life — books you've finished, ones you're in the middle of, and the pile you want to get to. Built for anyone who reads and wants to remember what they thought.

## What it should do

- Add a book with its title and author
- Mark each book as **want to read**, **reading**, or **read**
- Give finished books a rating (e.g. 1–5 stars)
- Jot down notes or thoughts on a book
- See your books grouped by their reading status
